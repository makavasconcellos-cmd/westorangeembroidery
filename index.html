About Us

A Family Business Serving Our Community

West Orange Embroidery started when we needed custom embroidered products for our own business and couldn't find a local shop that delivered the quality and service we were looking for. With my wife's background in sewing and design, we decided to do it ourselves — and quickly realized we could provide that same quality to other businesses in our community.

Nearly two years later, we're proud to serve Orlando-area companies, teams, and organizations from our shop in Winter Garden. We run two 10-needle embroidery machines, giving us the capacity to handle bulk orders while maintaining the personal attention that comes from being a true family operation.

When you work with us, you're working directly with the people doing the embroidery. No middlemen, no overseas production, no surprises. Just quality work from your neighbors in West Orange County.
