Frequently Asked Questions About Ocoee Embroidery

Can I stop by in person?
Yes! We're located in Winter Garden, just minutes from Ocoee. Stop by to discuss your project, see samples, and get a quote face-to-face. Call ahead if you'd like to schedule a time.
How fast can you turn around embroidery?
Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days. Rush orders (3-5 days) are available depending on current workload. Contact us about your timeline and we'll let you know what we can do.
What's the minimum order for Ocoee businesses?
20 pieces per design. Perfect for small businesses, teams, and anyone testing a design before committing to larger quantities.
Can I update or change a design after the first order?
Absolutely. Many of our Ocoee clients reorder the same design or request modifications. We make changes simple and can quickly reorder with new details.
Do you supply apparel or do I need to bring my own?
Either way works. You can bring your own apparel and we'll embroider it, or we can source quality blanks for you. Let us know your preference.
