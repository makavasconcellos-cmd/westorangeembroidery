Frequently Asked Questions About Windermere Embroidery

Can you work with premium fabrics?
Yes. We have experience embroidering on fine fabrics—cashmere blends, Egyptian cotton, silk-infused materials, and other luxury apparel. We understand how to maintain fabric integrity while creating beautiful embroidery.
Do you offer custom digitization for unique designs?
We digitize logos for embroidery. Bring us your design and we'll create a digitized version that translates beautifully into embroidery. We focus on precision and detail to match Windermere's standards.
Can you match specific colors or brands?
We'll get as close as possible using our extensive thread library. Embroidery thread won't be a perfect match to printed ink, but we show thread samples for approval before production—ensuring your brand colors look perfect.
Do you work with smaller orders for clubs and private organizations?
Yes. Our 20-piece minimum works well for country clubs, academies, and exclusive organizations. We maintain the same premium quality whether you need 20 pieces or 500.
How do I discuss my project in detail?
Many of our Windermere clients prefer in-person consultations. We're just 10 minutes away and happy to discuss your project face-to-face. Call or email to schedule a time.
