Frequently Asked Questions About Winter Garden Embroidery

What's the minimum order for embroidery?
Our minimum is 20 pieces per design. This works great for small businesses, departments, and anyone testing a design before committing to larger quantities. For larger orders, we offer competitive pricing and fast turnarounds.
How much does embroidery cost?
Pricing depends on several factors: design complexity, number of stitches, quantity, apparel type, and turnaround time. Get a free quote by filling out our form or stopping by in person. Most Winter Garden businesses find our pricing very competitive.
Can I supply my own apparel/garments?
Absolutely! Many customers bring their own polos, t-shirts, hats, or jackets. We'll embroider them with care. Just make sure garments are clean and ready to embroider. We can also source quality blanks for you if you prefer.
What's the difference between embroidery and screen printing?
Embroidery creates a raised, textured design using thread—it's durable, looks premium, and works great for corporate/professional settings. Screen printing uses ink and is better for designs with many colors or photographic images. We do embroidery; for screen printing, we can refer you to partners.
How long does embroidery take?
Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days. Rush orders (3-5 days) are available. Bring your logo, apparel, and timeline—we'll let you know if we can help.
Do you offer design services?
We're a production partner, not a design studio. Bring us your existing logo and branding, and we'll embroider it beautifully. If you need a logo designed, we can refer you to local designers we trust.
