Frequently Asked Questions About Orlando Embroidery

Can you handle conference/convention turnarounds?
Yes. Many Orlando conferences and conventions require merchandise on tight timelines. Contact us with your timeline and order details—we work with clients to meet deadline requirements whenever possible. Many of our Orlando corporate clients appreciate our flexibility and reliability.
Do you do rush orders?
Absolutely. Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days, but rush orders (3-5 days) are available depending on complexity and current workload. Let us know your timeline and we'll work with you.
What's your minimum order?
20 pieces per design. For large corporate orders in the hundreds or thousands, we offer bulk pricing and efficient production scheduling.
Can I bring my own apparel for embroidery?
Absolutely. Many Orlando businesses have preferred vendors for apparel blanks. Bring your shirts, hats, jackets, or bags, and we'll handle the embroidery. We can also source quality apparel on your behalf if you prefer a turnkey solution.
How do I get a quote for a large order?
Email us at info@westorangeembroidery.com or call with your project details: apparel type, quantity, design complexity, and timeline. We'll provide a detailed quote within one business day.
Can you handle multi-location corporate orders?
Yes. We work with multi-location Orlando businesses to ensure consistent branding across all sites. We can manage large-scale orders and maintain consistency across shipments.
